Native vs. Cross-Platform: Choosing the Right Approach for Your MVP

**Full Article Preview:** When launching a Minimum Viable Product (MVP), the choice between Native (Swift/Kotlin) and Cross-Platform (React Native/Flutter) is critical. Native offers the best performance and access to device-specific features but requires separate codebases. Cross-Platform allows for faster time-to-market and a unified codebase, ideal for simple business apps or consumer MVPs.
